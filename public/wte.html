What to Expect for the 16+ Night

Our 16+ night promises a more intense and thrilling experience. Here’s what you can expect:

Darker Themes

The tone is creepier, with unsettling, disturbing settings designed to heighten your sense of fear.

Jump Scares and Startles

Actors may surprise you with sudden appearances and creepy whispers, so be prepared for some heart-racing moments.

Close Encounters

Expect actors to get close, though they won’t touch you. However, you may feel—or be felt—by static props as you move through the house, adding a layer of unsettling realism.

Scarier Sound Effects

Prepare for loud, unsettling sounds—creaking doors, distant screams, and eerie noises that make you feel like something is lurking nearby.

Disturbing Props

Encounter disturbing imagery such as dead bodies, organs, and grotesque, unsettling scenes that push the comfort zone.
