1. What are the event dates and times?

The event will be held from 6:00 p.m. until 9:00 p.m. on October 26th and 27th. Time slots may vary, so check the registration page or city website for details.

2. Is there an age restriction?

October 26th: All ages are welcome.
October 27th: For ages 16 and up only, due to the intense nature of the event.

3. Is pre-registration required?

Registration is only required for October 27th, the night restricted to ages 16 and older. Participants will queue for going through on October 26th.

4. Where will the donations go?

All donations will benefit BEACON (Brunswick Ecumenical Assistance Committee on Needs) and will help fund future haunted house events.

5. Will food and drinks be available?

Yes, prepackaged food and drinks will be available for purchase. Proceeds will be added to the donations.

6. What happens if it rains?

The event is rain or shine, with both indoor and outdoor components, so come prepared for any weather!

7. What kind of special effects can I expect?

Special effects may be sudden and will engage all of your senses. Please be aware that the experience is designed to be frightening.

8. Is the event accessible for wheelchair users?

Yes, the haunted house is wheelchair accessible. However, please note that the special effects and atmosphere are designed to be intense and scary.

9. Can children attend?

Children are welcome on October 26th. However, due to the nature of the event, parental discretion is advised, and it is not recommended for very young children.

10. How can I make a donation?

Donations can be made at the event. If you're purchasing food or drinks, the proceeds will also go toward the donations.

11. How can I volunteer to help with the event?

If you'd like to help, you can sign up at hauntbrunswick.com to volunteer!

For more information or to register, please visit the City of Brunswick website or contact the event organizers under "Contact Us".
